About

I am a self-taught programmer who began my journey in the tech industry in late 2025. Hailing from Indonesia, I chose to build a solid foundational base before specializing in software development.

My learning approach is sequential and depth-first: mastering one fundamental concept thoroughly before moving on to the next level. I am currently learning frontend fundamentals (HTML5, CSS, vanilla JavaScript) and following a clear roadmap toward becoming a full-stack Web3 engineer.

My long-term career goal is to become a Web3/Blockchain Full-Stack Developer who is competitive in the global market, focusing on smart contract development and DeFi protocols. I recognize that a strong Web2 foundation (frontend and backend) is a prerequisite for seriously venturing into Web3.

I believe that the journey to becoming a competent developer is a marathon, not a sprint. Steady progress, good documentation, and a portfolio of real-world projects are the keys to success in this industry.
